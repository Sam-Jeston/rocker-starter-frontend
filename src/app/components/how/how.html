Two key signatures are available - A Major and A Minor. One of these is chosen at random.

Depending on the key signature chosen, a common chord progression for the generated track is selected. The current chord progression possibilities are:

Major Progressions

  • I - I - iv - V
  • I - iii - V - iv
  • I - IV - V - IV
  • iv - V - I - iii
  • I - V - vi - IV

Minor Progressions

  • i - III - VII - VII
  • i - v - iv - III
  • i - v - VII - VI
  • i - VI - VII - VII
  • i - VI - VII - i

A pre-recorded guitar and bass track that fit this progression are chosen at random. A random drum track is also included.

The melody is randomly generated based on the arpeggio on the current chord. The rhythm of the melody is also random.

And thats it! Programmitcally generated music snippets are created.
